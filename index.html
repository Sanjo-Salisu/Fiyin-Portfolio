About Me

I'm a digital Fashion illustrator, 3D dress modeler and a fashion designer. I merge creativity with tech by creating physical/virtual outfits and digital styling concepts using cutting-edge software. My work centers on storytelling through fashion, conscious styling and innovation, especially from an African perspective. I'm also passionate about teaching fashion skills and helping others understand sustainability in a relatable way.
